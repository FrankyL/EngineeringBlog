12 Buttons ESP32-based Macropad with Hot-swap sockets (V3-TFT variant)

Franky Laurentis January 13, 2025 ESP32

Improvement upon the V2.5 by using just 1 screen instead of 3 smaller ones

Used the ST7789 and it's a huge pain in the neck initially, It's only easier once the TFT_eSPI library works

It is much faster than the triple I2C OLEDs in V2.5 but still not sure how it will hold up against the 128x96 OLED. I'm indeed aware that it might be a waste using the TFT LCD to display just monochrome icons, but I haven't got the time to come up with a set of full color icons yet

The microSD card module is half the size of the one I used in V2.5 but I am genuinely annoyed at it jutting out like that.
