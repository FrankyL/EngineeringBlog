Smart Wall Switch (1 gangway &amp; 3 gangway variants)

Franky Laurentis November 25, 2024

Manufactured the PCBs from JLCPCB

3D printed casing and implemented RGB LED on PCB for adjustable night light,

ESP32 for MQTT control and a relay to control house lighting.

How the LEDs look like in the dark:

Controlling light switch using a random MQTT app found on Play Store and also using a GUI done with NodeRED:

Only ever built 1 of the triple gangway type, too bulky for my liking and will probably redesign with smaller relays

Wi-Fi SSID, passwords, color of RGB LEDs, MQTT name and channels all stored within EEPROM similar to the V1 and V2 macropads, and I honestly think it is way too troublesome to be an actual serious product to sell to the wider public.
